NEPAL

I traveled to Nepal in 2019. This small Himalayan nation enthralled me with its spirituality and natural beauty. I was surprised to see such varied landscapes, from lush, green forests in the foothills to snowy alpine peaks that made me feel extraordinarily small.

I loved the color and chaos of Kathmandu, the capital city, as well as the warmth of the locals. I met so many kind people who showed me stunning Buddhist temples and incredible Nepali cuisine. I would love to see more of this country one day.
