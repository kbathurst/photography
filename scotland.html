SCOTLAND

 

Scotland is otherworldly. I traveled there in October of 2016 and was mesmerized by the dramatic coastlines, imposing mountains, and fascinating castles.

I spent time in the beautiful and quaint city of Edinburgh, which I loved, but my favorite part of the country was the Highlands. It was there that I saw the impossibly stunning Isle of Skye, as well as the Ardnamurchan Peninsula, a remote place where I worked at a bed and breakfast.

Although the dark and chilly November I spent in Scotland wasn't peak season, it was incredibly lovely with the gentle winter sunlight and the rich hues of burgundy and gold dotting the landscape.
