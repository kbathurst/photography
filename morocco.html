MOROCCO

I went to Morocco by myself at the beginning of 2017. It took me a while to get used to the noise, chaos, and the impossible labyrinth of narrow streets and alleyways that made up the city centers.

However, after some time, I fell in love with this enchanting country. Its varied landscapes, from the red sand dunes of the Sahara to the tranquil Atlantic coast, are nothing short of stunning. The cities boast phenomenal palaces, as well as fascinating medinas full of intriguing sights and sounds - metalworkers, spice sellers, herbalists, fish mongers, and more.
