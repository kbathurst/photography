AMERICAN WEST

Even though I have been to many different places around the world, I think my own country might be the most beautiful.

The West, in particular, fascinates me with its diverse and sublime landscapes. There are endless things to discover, from the alien-like red rocks of the Southwest to the moss-covered forests of Northwest Washington.

I could never choose a favorite place from this half of the country, but I was especially impressed by sunrise at Bryce Canyon, sunset at Canyonlands, and an unforgettable moonrise over the sand dunes in Death Valley.
